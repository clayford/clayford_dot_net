Note: There's more happening in this song than my transcription
indicates. For instance, in the bridge John goes wild. I suggest you do
the same and not worry about playing what he played note-for-note. Also, 
I think John plays some chords in the chorus, but they're just too hard
for me to pick out in the mix. Likewise in the verse, if you listen
very carefully, you can sort of hear him tooling around. But again, I 
had a hard time hearing exactly what he was doing so I let it go. 

Despite  these shortcomings, I think this transcription holds water 
musically and works with the song. I'm also pretty sure I nailed 
everything from that kick-ass break that begins at 1:50, to the equally 
kick-ass lick before the final chorus.
